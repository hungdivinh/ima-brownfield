Brownfield Modification & Integration Works – BNAG, Bonny Island, Nigeria
Short Project Summary

The IMA Gas Field Development Project aims to develop offshore gas production and integrate it into the existing BNAG onshore facilities at Bonny Island, Nigeria. The brownfield scope includes engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning of new facilities, as well as modification and tie-in works to ensure seamless integration with existing BNAG infrastructure and export to NLNG.
